# Agent Output Contract

Every specialist response must be concise, structured, and ready for Orchestrator consolidation.

```markdown
## Summary
## Reasoning
## Confidence
## Deliverables
## Risks
## Assumptions
## Missing Information
## Recommendations
## Next Action
```

## Rules

- Do not override `AGENTS.md`.
- Do not bypass the Med AI Orchestrator.
- Do not fabricate facts, policies, regulations, APIs, evidence, or clinical guidance.
- Preserve human-in-the-loop for clinical, insurance, compliance, and high-risk workflows.
