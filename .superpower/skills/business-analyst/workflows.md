# Business Analyst Workflows

## Requirement Intake Workflow

1. Receive Orchestrator brief or stakeholder input.
2. Identify source, requester, business goal, workflow, and urgency.
3. Capture assumptions, constraints, and missing information.
4. Classify module impact across Med AI NexSure.
5. Decide whether clarification or specialist review is needed.

## Stakeholder Clarification Workflow

1. Restate the request in business language.
2. Ask for objective, users, trigger, expected outcome, and success measure.
3. Identify excluded behavior and governance constraints.
4. Confirm human review and audit expectations.
5. Update requirement confidence.

## Current State / Future State Analysis

1. Document current manual or system workflow.
2. Document target workflow.
3. Compare actors, data, rules, handoffs, and controls.
4. Identify gaps, risks, and dependencies.
5. Recommend next BA output.

## Gap Analysis Workflow

1. Define current state.
2. Define desired future state.
3. Identify capability, data, process, policy, and audit gaps.
4. Rate impact and urgency.
5. Recommend handoff owner.

## User Story Creation Workflow

1. Identify actor.
2. Identify capability.
3. Identify business outcome.
4. Add acceptance criteria.
5. Add traceability and handoff.

## Acceptance Criteria Workflow

1. Start from observable behavior.
2. Write Given / When / Then criteria.
3. Add edge, error, empty, audit, and low-confidence cases.
4. Confirm QA can test it.
5. Confirm safety and compliance boundaries.

## Business Rule Extraction Workflow

1. Identify policy, workflow, validation, or decision rule.
2. Define owner and source.
3. Define condition, action, exception, and audit requirement.
4. Route clinical, insurance, compliance, or architecture rules to specialists.

## Requirement Validation Workflow

1. Run requirement quality checklist.
2. Check conflicts with existing workflows.
3. Check missing information and ambiguity.
4. Check safety, PDPA, OIC, audit, and governance constraints.
5. Prepare handoff package.

## Cross-Agent Handoff Workflow

1. Summarize requirement and confidence.
2. Split handoff by agent ownership.
3. Include open questions, risks, and required decisions.
4. Preserve traceability to source requirement.
5. Send to Orchestrator for delegation.

## Change Request Workflow

1. Capture requested change and reason.
2. Identify affected modules, requirements, tests, data, and audit logs.
3. Assess gap, impact, risk, and MVP effect.
4. Route prioritization to Product Owner.
5. Update traceability and decision log.

## Module Alignment

Apply these workflows to Patient Management, Visit Management, SOAP Note, AI Clinical Engine, Diagnosis, Prescription, Medical Certificate, Claim Readiness, Evidence Package, Insurance Intelligence, Economic Intelligence, Audit & Compliance, and User Management.
